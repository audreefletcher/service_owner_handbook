The business value of design

A useful article on the ROI of design (really in the context of service design and agile multi-disciplinary teams). So often policy colleagues struggle to understand the value of user-centred design - either seeing it as making things look "pretty" or seeing "user experience" as something fluffy and nice-to-have. McKinsey have gone to quite a lot of effort to show that those orgs that invest in proper user-centred design capability get outsized outcomes.


Liberating structures

A really great set of alternative approaches for facilitating more open meetings and conversations. Highly recommended where you don't want an overstructured session but want to make sure you still get something at the end.


Measuring what matters

A video on why and how to use OKRs (Objectives and Key Results) as a goal-setting and performance management framework. OKRs help your team understand what success looks like and how to measure it - so that you can be confident that you're progressing in the short-term towards your desired medium-to-long-term destination.
