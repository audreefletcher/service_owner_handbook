Design Histories

We're huge fans of design histories like these so we thought I'd share an example with you today - the one built by the Becoming A Teacher team. Design histories are important for knowledge management - showing and evidencing design decisions that have been made. They're good for communication - telling the story of your service as it is evolving. They're good for audit and archive - because they're connected to either actual code or real screenshots and so are a means of navigating "the public record" of what was, needed for accountability. And the latter is becoming increasingly important - we're expecting significant pressure for us to become more open in our designs and code over the coming year. Anyway, take a look, see what you think. The code is, of course, open and cloneable on Github.
