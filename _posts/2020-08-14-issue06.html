The true cost of running live services

Failing to properly budget for live services is a widespread problem across government. Partly because people don't have good mental models for what happens in live; and partly because of the funding classification chasm we have to cross when transitioning from Public Beta. I know you'll be fine with the former (read the article anyway) - and don't forget to look at your spending assumptions over the next SR period (including programme/admin/capital balance evolving over time) when putting in your SR bids.
