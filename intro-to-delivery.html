Our service owner model

In DfE we have moved past traditional civil service methods of working and have developed the service ownership model. This brings multidisciplinary teams - policy and delivery teams - together to deliver better services and better policy under a Service Owner Model.

Where possible, we have one service owner who is responsible for all of it, for the policy and the service that users experience it through. That way there’s no divide between policy and delivery; or between delivery and the maintenance and improvement of the live services.

We are one team.

No matter where you are working within it. What matters is meeting user needs by making sure that policy and service objectives can adapt and respond to user research. You can read more about the Service Owner Model and how we make it work in this blog post.
